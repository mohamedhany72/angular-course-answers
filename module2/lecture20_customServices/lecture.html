Custom services

custom services are used to share data between controllers, the data inside services are "singlton" which means they are only institiated one time, and if the services is never invoked it won't be institiated.

here we have four controllers the first two are "function constructors" with controller as syntax and are linked with the same service, the second two are normal controllers with $scope injected and are connected with a differnt service.
theres a fifth controller called "shopListShowCtrl3" which shows that the same service can be used between different types of controllers.
